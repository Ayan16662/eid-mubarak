— A Heartfelt Message —

Blessed Eid to All

✦ ☪ ✦
كُلُّ عَامٍ وَأَنْتُمْ بِخَيْرٍ

May the divine light of Eid fill your heart with
joy, your home with warmth,
and your life with love and laughter.

On this sacred day, as families gather and prayers ascend,
may Allah shower you with endless blessings
and grant all your heartfelt wishes.

— With Love & Prayers, Eid Mubarak 1446H —
